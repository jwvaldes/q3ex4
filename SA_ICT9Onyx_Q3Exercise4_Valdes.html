    • Hong Kong


    Hong Kong is a city in the country of China. It is known for being one of the most densely populated places in the world!



    What did Jaz do there?

    My family and I stayed at the Panda Hotel!

    It's a very good hotel from my experience! It had outdoor pools and the rooms were clean and pretty! I guess what's most memorable from my experience there is that me and my siblings each got a free tiny stuffed panda once it was time to leave. They were adorable!


    We didn't spend a lot of our days staying at the hotel, though! Because we visited the Hong Kong Disneyland!


    I may not remember much, since my visit was long ago in the year of 2017. I do remember having the most fun there! We ate lots of sweets and went to so much rides! Although I also remember being disappointed at the waffles... *shivers*


    We also visited lots of malls, although mostly only ate at familiar fast-food chains since we were familiar with the menu and it was more likely for the people who work there to know how to speak English.


    That's it for my Hong Kong travel blog entry! Let's meet again at the next destination! <3
